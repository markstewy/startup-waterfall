About Seedval

SeedVal is a simplified waterfall calculator that visualizes traditional VC and Angel investments terms. The purpose of SeedVal is to help technical founders protect their equity by understanding the confusing legal terms in investment docs.

The current waterfall assumes a pre money cap table with nothing but common shares. Options, convertible debt, SAFE agreements or multiple preferred rounds are not considered.

If you find this tool helpful let me know in the feedback section. I may add on more complexity for multiple rounds and different investment types if there is enough interest.
